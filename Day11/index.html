Why Candy?

Today, I wanted to do a couple of things: I wanted to try using custom fonts, and I wanted to make an icon. This candy was a simple one I made to play around with highlights and shadows. The neat thing about candy as a theme is the pastel color palette, which is making a comeback in web design and happens to have some of my favorite colors to work with.

One thing that I particularly wanted to accomplish with the candy icon was to make an iOS Springboard icon that I wouldn't mind actually shipping with, and I think I've finally managed to get one! Since it isn't being used on an App Store app, I added the rounded corners and shine myself - you can see the original here.
