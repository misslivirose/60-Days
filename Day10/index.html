College of Engineering

I earned my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in May of 2013. During my time at Tech, I was an active member of the Society of Women Engineers and am a sister of the engineering sorority Alpha Omega Epsilon. I participated in the first-year Women in Engineering community housing at Virginia Tech and served as a mentor with the Center for the Enhancement of Engineering Diversity as well as for the Computer Science department.

After graduation, I joined Microsoft as a Program Manager in user experience for the Remote Desktop team in Mountain View, California, where I have been working UI design and development for the iOS, Android, Windows Phone, and Mac RDP clients.
